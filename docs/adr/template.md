## Context

What problem are we solving? What forces are at play? What constraints
exist? Describe the situation neutrally — someone reading this in two
years should understand the world we were operating in.

## Decision

What did we decide? State it clearly and concisely.

## Alternatives Considered

What else did we look at? For each alternative, briefly explain why we
didn't pick it. This is the most important section — it proves we
thought about tradeoffs instead of defaulting to the first idea.

## Consequences

**Positive:**

- What becomes easier or better because of this decision?

**Negative:**

- What becomes harder? What are we giving up?
- What new risks or complexities are we introducing?

**At higher scale we would consider:**

- What's the next evolution of this decision if requirements grow?
